Our Production Process "BEA TEE AGRO PROCESSING"


Introduction

At BEATEE AGRO-PROCESSING, every grain of Gari is produced with care, precision, and passion. We combine traditional Ghanaian processing methods with modern hygiene standards to create a product that is both authentic and safe for consumption. Our production process is designed to preserve the natural taste and nutrients of cassava, while ensuring consistency in texture, color, and flavor. Each stage from the farm to the final packaging follows a strict quality control system, guaranteeing that our Gari remains the Bringer of Joy it is known to be.

Step 1: Harvesting the Cassava

We begin by sourcing fresh and mature cassava roots from trusted local farmers. Our commitment to using only the best raw materials ensures that the cassava we process is free from rot, dirt, or chemical residue. We support local farmers by maintaining fair and long-term partnerships that empower communities and promote sustainable agriculture.

Step 2: Peeling and Washing

Once the cassava roots arrive at our facility, they are thoroughly peeled and washed using clean water and modern washing systems. This step removes all sand, soil, and impurities to guarantee a hygienic foundation for the rest of the process. We believe that cleanliness at this stage sets the tone for a superior final product.

Peeling I

Peeling II

Step 3: Grating

The clean cassava is then mechanically grated into a smooth, uniform pulp. Grating breaks down the cassava and prepares it for fermentation. Our machines are designed to achieve the perfect consistency while minimizing waste.

Grating

Grating

Step 4: Fermentation

The grated cassava is packed into fermentation sacks and left to ferment for 3-5 hours. This natural fermentation process enhances the flavor, aroma, and texture of the Gari, giving it that authentic Ghanaian taste everyone loves. We monitor the process closely to ensure proper timing and temperature for the best results.

Fermentation

Step 5: Pressing

After fermentation, the cassava mash is pressed to remove excess water using hydraulic or manual pressing machines. This step reduces moisture content and ensures the Gari is dry enough for roasting. The pressing process is handled with care to maintain the right balance between dryness and softness.

Pressing

After Pressing

Step 6: Sieving

The pressed cassava cake is then sieved to remove lumps and fibrous materials. Sieving gives the Gari its fine and consistent grain, ensuring smoothness and easy roasting.

Fermentation

Step 7: Roasting

This is where the magic happens. The sieved cassava is roasted in large, clean pans under steady heat until it turns into crispy, golden Gari. Our skilled roasters use traditional techniques, perfected through experience, to control the temperature and timing. This step gives our Gari its unique aroma, rich taste, and satisfying crunch.

Roasting

Step 8: Cooling, Drying and Sieving

After roasting, the Gari is allowed to cool naturally in a clean, dry environment. This helps prevent moisture buildup and preserves the crisp texture. We never rush this process patience ensures lasting freshness. After the cooling and drying stage, our Gari goes through a second sieving process to ensure superior smoothness and purity. This stage helps us separate the coarser particles or larger granules from the fine, even-textured Gari that customers love. The Gari that passes through this second sieving is what gives BEA TEE AGRO PROCESSING its distinct texture light, smooth, and easy to soak or prepare.

This extra care step guarantees:

(1) A more uniform grain size for better quality.
(2) Improved texture that feels smooth on the tongue.
(3) Cleaner appearance and longer shelf life.
(4) Consistency in every pack from the first spoon to the last.

Sieving

Step 9: Packaging

Once cooled, the Gari is carefully weighed, sealed, and packaged in food-grade bags that protect it from air and moisture. Our packaging is designed to preserve freshness, extend shelf life, and make the product attractive to customers. We offer packaging in 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, and 25kg sizes, suitable for homes, shops, and bulk distributors.

Packaging

Step 10: Quality Inspection

Before any product leaves our factory, it goes through a final quality inspection. We check for taste, texture, moisture content, and packaging integrity. Only Gari that meets our strict standards earns the BEA TEE AGRO PROCESSING label your assurance of pure quality and joy.

Final Message

At BEA TEE AGRO PROCESSING, every step of our process reflects our belief that quality food begins with care. From the farmer’s field to your dining table, we maintain the highest standards of hygiene, honesty, and dedication. That’s why our Gari doesn’t just feed it brings joy to every home.
